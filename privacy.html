Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

The short version: We collect minimal data. Your patient data never touches our servers. Prompts sync, reports stay local on your device.

1. Who We Are

HelpMeReport is operated by João Abrantes, based in Porto, Portugal. We provide voice-to-report AI prompt tools for radiologists.

Contact: hello@helpmereport.com

2. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address, name (optional), and payment details (processed by Stripe)
  • Usage data: Which prompts you download, sync timestamps, and basic analytics
  • Device information: Device identifiers for prompt sync functionality

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We never collect, store, or process:

  • Patient data or protected health information (PHI)
  • Radiology reports or dictations
  • Medical images or DICOM files
  • Voice recordings

Our prompts are templates that run locally on your device. The AI processing happens on your machine through your voice dictation software - we only sync the prompt templates themselves.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service updates and product announcements (you can opt out)
  • Respond to support requests

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure in the European Union. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe - we never see or store your full card details.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (EU region)
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Basic analytics: Page views and feature usage (no personal tracking)

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As we're based in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Object: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email hello@helpmereport.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law (e.g., payment records for tax purposes).

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential: Authentication and session management
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal tracking)

No advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@helpmereport.com.
